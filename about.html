About Bumi Reflesia

Raflesia Restaurant was established in 2025 as an expression of love for Bengkulu's rich culinary heritage. The name Raflesia was inspired by the Rafflesia arnoldii flower, the largest flower in the world and an icon of Bengkulu Province. The restaurant was founded based on the founder's desire to preserve family recipes that had been passed down from generation to generation. Many of Bengkulu's signature dishes are becoming increasingly rare, so the restaurant is committed to making them more widely known and loved again, especially by the younger generation.

Meet Our Chef

Chef Juna

Chef Juna is a professional chef from Bengkulu who is known for his expertise in preparing traditional dishes with modern kitchen standards. With experience in various national restaurants, he brings his expertise to preserve the authentic flavors of Bengkulu while delivering professional cooking techniques and quality. Currently, he serves as the Head Chef at Raflesia Restaurant, ensuring that every dish is served with the best flavor and high consistency.
